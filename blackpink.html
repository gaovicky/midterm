Support from Bandmates

Rosé's BLACKPINK bandmates, Jennie, Jisoo, and Lisa were all very supportive throughout her solo debut. In a press conference, Rosé revealed, "Each one of them really cheered me on so much. It just made me realize how important encouragement was."

Jisoo

Jisoo visited Rosé when she was filming the music video for "On the Ground." Jisoo also tuned in to Rosé's live broadcast on Navier Live. On March 14th Korea's White Day), Jisoo sent Rosé custom made "-R-" lollipop and candy set.

"BLACKPINK’s pride, our voice genius, Rosé. Chaengie! You’ve waited long for your first solo, so do everything you want and I hope you make many happy memories with BLINKs."
-Jisoo

Jennie

Jennie and Rosé calls each other often. Jennie also sent Rosé cute gifts such as a pink rose plushie and jewelry with Rosé's puppy's name, Hank, on it.

"Hi Rosie posie, finally today has come. Congratulations, I’m so so happy for you! I know how long you’ve been waiting to release your magical voice to the world and since I had the privilege to hear the songs first, I know everybody is going to love it. Don’t forget to take care of yourself, your body, and Hank.
-Jennie

Lisa

Lisa was very excited about album -R-. After watching Rosé's music video, Lisa went around and told her staff members that the M/V was 'so cool.' Lisa also went down to SBS "Inkigayo" to support Rosé give her a bouquet of flowers with a note saying: "From your twin sister, Lili, congratulations on the first broadcast, love you."

"Please give lots of love to
cutie Rosé’s solo, my twin."
-Lisa
