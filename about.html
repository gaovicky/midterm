Who is Rosé?

Born in February 11, 1997 in Auckland, New Zealand, Roseanne Park also known as Rosé is the main vocalist and lead dancer of BLACKPINK, a South Korean girl group. She has been acknowledged in the K-Pop industry for having a distinct vocal timbre and a wide vocal range. She’s the second member of BLACKPINK to debut solo.

"When you just feel the most hollow, life gives you sweets."
-Rosé

Rosé’s debut solo single album -R- was released on March 12, 2021. This album allowed the K-Pop singer to reflect where she has come and to set a reminder of what motivates her. In BLACKPINK’s 2020 Netflix documentary ‘Light Up The Sky,’ Rosé revealed she worked up the courage to be vulnerable and write something on her own.

She explains that her lead track "On The Ground" captures her journey from being a trainee to being a member of BLACKPINK. Her other song, “Gone,” echoes the pain she experienced during a difficult breakup.

She hoped -R- would cheer up her fans during the pandemic.
